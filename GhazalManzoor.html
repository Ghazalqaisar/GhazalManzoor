About Us

Telecommunication is a huge market and to get a share in this market iShare AB will focus on general customers, toll-free, prepaid card, prepaid mobile data cards, and international travelers. The primary target of iShare AB will be public that needs the essential telecom services such as call, SMS, email services. The marketing department will work on to develop and initiate plans to promote different service of iSahre according to the needs of the customers at low cost and efforts will be done to meet the marketing goals of the company.

The marketing team will work closely with the BI team to analyze the market needs and develop plans to create different packages for different customers and market need. The Marketing team will create and develop internet ads, fliers, brochures, and media advertisements. The team will also organize the events to promote the company products.

The overview of the sales of new stores across US, the monthy sales of different districts, and the analysis of the the new stores can be seen from the Power BI dashboard below.



Services

iShare AB will develop and deliver the personal communication and messaging services for individuals at the first stage. The customers can buy the prepaid SIM and top up a card from the major retailers in the country and can buy the card online from the company website.

Description of Products and Services: Customers can use the iShare AB services from any mobile phones and can send and receive calls, text and video massages to friends and family at an affordable cost. Other services offered my iShare will be, call forwarding, billing services, voice messaging. In future and after the upgradation of the systems, iShare AB has plans add more products and services such as, call waiting, conference calling, wakeup call services, email, and fax services.
